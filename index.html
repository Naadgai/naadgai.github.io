About Us

From Mongolia to the Global Market

Nuudelchin Studio LLC is a mobile game development company based in Mongolia , developing mobile games for the global market. The name “Nuudelchin” means nomad in the Mongolian language, reflecting our spirit of adaptability, creativity, and global reach.

Founded in 2026, our team consists of experienced professionals with over 10 years of experience in the gaming industry. We continue to grow by bringing in talented individuals who share our passion for building engaging digital experiences.

Our mission is to create high-quality games that deliver fun, immersive, and meaningful experiences to players worldwide. We are committed to developing innovative and enjoyable games that can be appreciated by people of all ages and backgrounds.
